About Kamal & Co. Advocates
Kamal & Co. Advocates was established in the year 1998 with the notion of “entire legal service” for litigants under one roof. From the very beginning Kamal & Co. Advocates followed a People Oriented approach and adopted highest level of professionalism and integrity. With the wide practical knowledge and rich experience, our advocates provide comprehensive legal solutions for its clients. We continue to follow the principle of “client-first” in providing reliable and highly effective legal services. We trust, we have a significant role in leading our clients along the perilous road to justice; making the journey a smooth sail. We trust in giving our client a voice and ensuring that they are effectively heard.

Our innovative legal approach is driven by the Founder, Adv. Kamaluddin Ahmad.

Throughout the journey our founder has made sure that, our firm provides personalised approach to legal representations by listening to their respective needs and provide creative, effective and practical solutions.

After our continuous service in Bangalore providing highest client satisfaction, we are soon extending our legal services and opening branches at other metropolitan cities in India.
Kamal & Co. is a known as a Top Lawyers in India with 30+ years of experience in Civil, Criminal, Family, Real Estate, Corporate & marital laws. We are renowned lawyers having expertise in High Court & Supreme Court of India litigations. Started in 1998, we are based in Bangalore, Hyderabad & Delhi. Consult our legal experts today.
